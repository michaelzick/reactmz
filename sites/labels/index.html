Patchbay Labeler!

by Michael Zick

To use:

Change the width and height by entering a value into the fields below.
Don't worry about keeping the "px" by each number, as this won't matter when entered.


You can also drag the bottom right corner of the patchbay picture to resize.


*Warning! If you refresh the browser before saving as Excel, your changes will be lost!
